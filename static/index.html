What we're building

Icarus is a social platform for artists, musicians, and filmmakers who believe creativity should come from people, not algorithms.

We verify that every piece of content is human-made. No AI art. No AI music. No AI videos. Just real work from real people.

The problem

Your feed is full of things that were never felt

Every platform you use is being flooded with AI-generated content. Art that took seconds to prompt. Music composed by machines. Videos stitched together without a human ever touching them.

Your work—the thing you spent hours on, the thing you actually care about—gets buried underneath it all.

The platforms don't mind. They profit from volume. They don't care if what fills your feed was made by a person or a prompt.

We think that's a problem worth solving.

Our principles

What we believe

01

Art costs something

Real creativity requires time, effort, and vulnerability. That's what makes it meaningful. We want to protect spaces where that work is valued.

02

Humans deserve credit

When you make something, people should know it came from you. Your work shouldn't be indistinguishable from something generated in seconds.

03

Community over content

We're not optimizing for engagement. We're building a place where creators can find each other and connect over shared craft.

"

Like Icarus, we believe in the audacity of human ambition—the sweat, the struggle, the soul that goes into making something real. In a world filling up with synthetic content, we're building a refuge for people who still believe art should cost something to create.

3

Connect with other creators who care about craft. People who understand what it takes to make something real.
